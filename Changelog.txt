-- V 3.0 beta (unofficial) Aug 31 2022 --
Author:
  lantaren@aim.com
  https://lantaren.bandcamp.com/ (Mostly electronic music these days)
Notes:
  Made a couple improvements over the last version.
  This is a clean / safe build, I decompiled the .exe and made changes as necessary to add the below features.
Features:
 - MP3 encoding quality selection:
   - V2 encodes mp3 to 128kbps. This is fine, but does not have the best audio quality for a full mix.
   - uses the following LAME encoder presets:
     - standard (~190kbps, default)
     - extreme (~240kbps)
     - intense (320kbps, recommended if you plan to do additional work with the files)
 - output directory selection
   - still defaults to \exportedSongs, but can be changed to better reflect your workflow
   - automatically opens output directory after conversion
Future:
  Not currently planning on continuing making edits to this software, however I may look into the following:
  - Volume of individual tracks and master volume
  - individual instrument EQ?
  - effects?
  - expose panning spread parameter
  - track / settings preview
  Almost all of these I feel are better suited to a DAW than to be built in here.
